Situation Update

The diplomatic situation has deteriorated sharply in the 1 day since the previous briefing. If the US tacitly accepts Iran's Hormuz gatekeeping role by failing to reopen the Strait militarily (Project Freedom already paused), Iran's deterrence posture is validated regardless of whether a deal is signed. Axis of Resistance protection remains non-negotiable: the explicit demand for end of hostilities "especially in Lebanon" makes Hezbollah's survival a precondition. Economic pressure is real — sanctions relief and asset unfreezing were prominent demands — but the packaging within maximalist terms suggests Tehran is willing to endure continued economic pain rather than accept terms that look like capitulation.
